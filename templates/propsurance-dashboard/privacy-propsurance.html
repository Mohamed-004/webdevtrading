    Privacy Policy

    Introduction

    Welcome to PropPatrol, the dedicated warranty provider for proprietary firm traders. We offer financial assurance to traders committed to proprietary firms, helping to safeguard their interests and financial investments in the trading environment.

    Information Collection

    We collect the following categories of personal information:

    • Personal Identification Information: This includes your name, address, and phone number.
    • Contact Details: We collect your email address to communicate with you effectively.
    • Payment Information: We collect card details required to process payments for our services.
    • Investor Password: We require your investor password from your proprietary firm to facilitate risk assessments and other necessary services.

    Purpose of Data Collection

    The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

    • Underwriting and managing insurance policies
    • Processing claims
    • Conducting risk assessments and fraud prevention activities
    • Ensuring compliance with our legal obligations

    Legal Basis for Processing

    The processing of your personal data is based on several legal grounds:

    • Consent: We process certain data based on your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
    • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of our insurance contracts with you.
    • Legal Obligations: We are required to process certain information in compliance with the law.
    • Legitimate Interests: Processing is also based on our legitimate interests in conducting and managing our business to enable us to give you the best service/products and secure experience.

Our security policies are regularly reviewed and updated to ensure we are in line with best practices and legal requirements.

    Rights of Data Subjects

    You have several rights regarding your personal data, including:

    • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
    • Correction: You have the right to ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

    • Restriction: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
    • Portability: You have the right to receive the personal data you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
