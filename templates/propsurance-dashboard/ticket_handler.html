    General FAQ

    General

    What is PropSurance?

      Propsurance is a risk mitigation service provider that ensures traders are compensated in the event of incidents as per the program. The coverage information for each plan: Trade Shield Gold, Bronze, and Silver —is clearly designated, providing tailored protection based on the selected level.

    General

    How does PropSurance work?

      Once a trader has a live account and opens a claim, they will be reimbursed based on the selected plan and the applicable claims or reasons as per their program. The reimbursement is provided according to the coverage information designated for their chosen plan—Trade Shield Gold, Bronze, or Silver.

    General

    What types of Plans are available?

      Propsurance offers three types of plans: Trade Shield Silver, Bronze, and Gold. Each plan has its own extensive coverage options and provides reimbursements tailored to the specific needs and circumstances of the trader.

    General

    What's my Maximum Allocated Account Amount?

      The maximum allocated account amount is $50,000. This means you can insure your trading accounts across different firms, as long as the total guaranteed amount doesn't exceed $50,000 For example: You can purchase a $25,000 insurance plan for an account at one firm and still be eligible for a separate $25,000 account at another firm, totaling $50,000 in coverage. *This only applies to the PropSurance Free denied payout coverage.*

    General

    What is my Investor Password & ID?

      Your investor password and ID are read-only credentials that allow us to securely connect to your account and monitor changes, such as your current phase progression. It's important to understand that these credentials do not grant us trading access. To maintain accurate coverage and avoid potential voiding of your Propsurance status, providing this information is mandatory.

    General

    What are the Payment Options?

      We currently accept and process payments through Stripe.

    General

    Can I insure an account whose Payout was already rejected?

      No, we do not allow an account to be insured retroactively before registering it with Prop Patrol. This is because the account was not insured at the time the payout was rejected, so it is not eligible for insurance.
