    General FAQ

    General

    What is PropSurance?

      Propsurance is a service provider that guarantees the rightful withdrawal of your trading profits through prop firms, in the event it is unrightfully rejected.

    General

    How does PropSurance work?

      In light of the prevailing dishonesty within the industry, Propsurance offers traders a guarantee that their hard-earned profits will be safely secured. With your Propsurance plan, you will be reimbursed—fully or partially—for any unjustly withheld payouts from our approved firms.

    General

    Can I insure an account whose Payout was already rejected?

      No, we do not allow an account to be insured retroactively before registering it with Prop Patrol. This is because the account was not insured at the time the payout was rejected, so it is not eligible for insurance. Additionally, if you insure a new account and experience a rejected payout, You must inform us of any rejected payouts only after the initial 30-day threshold period has passed following the insurance of your new account.

    General

    What types of Plans are available?

      Based on their extensive case history and the valuation of your account with the firm, PropPatrol customizes pricing to align with their reputation. Navigate to the Pricing dashboard for more info.

    General

    What's my Maximum Allocated Account Amount?

      The maximum allocated account amount is $200,000. This means you can insure your trading accounts across different firms, as long as the total guaranteed amount doesn't exceed $200,000 For example: You can purchase a $50,000 insurance plan for an account at one firm and still be eligible for a separate $150,000 account at another firm, totaling $200,000 in coverage.

    General

    What is my Investor Password & ID?

      Your investor password and ID are read-only credentials that allow us to securely connect to your account and monitor changes, such as your current phase progression. It's important to understand that these credentials do not grant us trading access. To maintain accurate coverage and avoid potential voiding of your Propsurance status, providing this information is mandatory.

    General

    What are the Payment Options?

      We currently accept and process payments through Stripe.

    PropSurance Users FAQ

    PropSurance User

    Current Coverage Rate?

      Upon signing up, your payout coverage is initially set at 40%. With each successful payout from a firm, your insurance rate increases by 20%. After three payouts, your insurance coverage reaches 100%. We currently limit processed payouts to a maximum of 10% of your account size at any given time; i.e $20,000 for a $20,0000 account However, for users who have already had 3 payouts processed through Propsurance, each successful payout thereafter will incrementally increase the maximum payout cap by 5%.

    PropSurance User

    Passed Your Current Stage?

      To maintain your Propsurance coverage, promptly update your credentials by submitting a case for "Passed Current Stage" and providing the necessary information. Failure to update your credentials promptly after being informed may result in the termination of your Propsurance benefits.

    PropSurance User

    Received Your Payout?

      It's important to update us after you receive a payout. Otherwise, our system may flag your account as invalid and void your PropSurance status.You can do so by opening a claim for "Received Payout" and filling out your information accordingly.

    PropSurance User

    My Account Dashboard says "Account Invalid"?

      • -Recent Payout or Stage Completion: You may have recently received a payout or completed a stage with your prop firm & not updated the status in PropSurance Dashboard.
      • -Inactive Account Update: You might not have updated your account status within
        two weeks from the event of the status change.
      • -How to Resolve: Open a claim through the "Account Invalid" option > Submit the necessary information promptly.
      • -Failure to Resolve Promptly: Failure to update your status after multiple attempts to reach you may result in the suspension or termination of your Propsurance coverage. We want to ensure your coverage remains active, so please take action promptly.

    PropSurance User

    Am I still entitled to a payout if I violate a firm's policy?

      Yes, however, you must have previously processed at least 3 successful payouts with us where we received the standard 5% fee from each. In this case, due to the nature of the violation, you will be entitled to a 40% coverage on the rejected payout, rather than the usual coverage.
